Nuestra Historia

Born in Oaxaca, Raised in KC

Three generations of family recipes, one mission: bring the bold, layered flavors of authentic Mexican street food to Kansas City. Every tortilla is hand-pressed. Every salsa is made fresh. Every taco tells a story.

We pop up at breweries, markets, and community events across the metro — chasing the people who appreciate real food made with real love.
